Running

One of my favorite activities to do is to go running. It is a great way to stay in shape and a good stress reliever. As a competitive high school runner, I naturally continued into college, joining the Purdue Run Club (August 2016 - May 2019) where I would invest 7-8 hours weekly training with the club and on top of traveling throughout the Midwest to compete against other college teams and clubs. My favorite event was and still is definitely the half marathon, where I have run 1:19:26, 1:23:22, and 1:17:36. Although I no longer train competitively, I still try to lace up everyday and run a couple of miles.
