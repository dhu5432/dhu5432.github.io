Baking

I'm always searching out new recipes to make. I started to bake semi-seriously in January of 2019 and have made many (although not all successfully) different desserts and dishes. It definitely taps into an inquisitive and geeky side of me because I feel like a scientist when I bake, measuring and proofing and analyzing and testing how each ingredient plays a vital role in the final dish. So far, I have made key lime cupcakes, chocolate chip cookies, tiramisu, apple roses, garlic knots, macarons, and so much more.
